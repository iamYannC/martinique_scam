In February 2026, I was urgently looking for accommodation in Martinique. Under pressure to find somewhere quickly, I responded to what seemed like a legitimate Facebook rental listing — and I let that desperation cloud my judgment. What followed was five days of calculated manipulation by a coordinated group who specifically target people in that situation. Instead, I found myself standing in front of a false address, luggage at my feet, in the midday heat — with no apartment and no money.
